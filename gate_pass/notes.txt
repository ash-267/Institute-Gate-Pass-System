Design and develop a Campus Digital Gate Pass & Visitor Management System                                                                                 

1)Design and Development – Develop a Campus Digital Gate Pass & Visitor Management System to replace manual visitor registers with a secure digital system.


2)Visitor Information Management – Record visitor details such as name, contact number, ID proof, purpose of visit, host details (faculty/student), visit date and time.


3)Digital Approval and Entry–Exit Tracking – Provide digital approval for visits and track visitor entry and exit status in real time.


4)Monitoring and Record Management – Allow security staff and administrators to monitor visitors inside the campus and manage records through a centralized database.


5)Report Generation and Security Control – Generate reports for security and administrative purposes while handling cases like missing exit entries, repeat visitors, and emergency visitor tracing.
